The Beginning

A Dream Born
in the Mountains

Bon Café & Sweets was born from a simple but powerful dream — to create a space in Pithoragarh where great food meets genuine warmth. Nestled on Rai, Dharchula Road, our café opened its doors to the mountains and never looked back.

What started as a small café serving gourmet burgers and fresh juices quickly grew into something much bigger. Word spread about our Bon Special Burger, our rich milkshakes, and the unforgettable birthday setups we curated with heart.

Today, Bon Café & Sweets is more than just a restaurant — it's a celebration hub. From birthday parties with custom décor to private dining for anniversaries, from hot sizzling rolls to melt-in-your-mouth Indian sweets — we pour love into everything we serve.

We believe every meal should feel special. Every visit should feel like coming home. And every celebration should be absolutely unforgettable.

Our Journey

How It All Happened

The Beginning
Bon Café Opens Its Doors
Started with a small menu of gourmet burgers and fresh beverages on Dharchula Road. The response from Pithoragarh was overwhelming from day one.
First Year
First 100 Birthdays Celebrated
Word spread about our special birthday setups. We hit our first 100 birthday celebrations — complete with custom décor, cakes, and memories that last forever.
Expanding
Menu Grows — Sweets & More
We expanded our menu to include authentic Indian sweets, rolls, pizza, and milkshakes. Bon Café & Sweets truly became the one-stop destination.
Private Dining
Rooms & Halls Launch
We opened private dining spaces for intimate dinners, corporate gatherings, and large birthday halls to accommodate Pithoragarh's growing love for us.
Today
500+ Celebrations & Still Growing
With 500+ birthdays celebrated, 5000+ happy customers, and an ever-growing menu — Bon Café & Sweets is Pithoragarh's most beloved destination. And the best is yet to come.
